# M10 Epic 3 Retrospective

## What Worked

- Existing semantic panels were enough for the first reasoning UX.
- Theia AI foundation can be added without handing it semantic authority.

## What Needed Tightening

- Panel actions had to remain narrow; a chat-first redesign would have blown scope.

## Carry Forward

- Keep reasoning close to diagnostics, review, and inspection context.
- Keep decision state visible in-place instead of burying it in background state.
