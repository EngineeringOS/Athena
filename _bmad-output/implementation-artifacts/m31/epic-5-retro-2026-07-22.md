## Epic Review

### Outcome

Epic 5 completed the M31 customer proof. The sample project now lives under
`examples/m31/sample-project`, the product smoke opens that exact sample, structured proof verifies
the create-connect-switch-reveal-reopen workflow, screenshot evidence supports human review, and

### What Worked

- The customer sample stayed semantic: nested ports and grouped connections are source readability
  features, not visual geometry or QET import shortcuts.
- Product smoke stayed structured-proof-first. Screenshots prove human-inspectable output, while
  semantic ids, transactions, Revision Guard, routes, sheets, references, lifecycle, and visual
  invariants are asserted as data.
- The final purge ledger prevented stale items from hiding in "done" stories. Open items were
  resolved, reclassified, or transferred to M32 with owner and verification.
- The `.tools` guard stayed explicit so accidental tool-session artifacts do not enter commit scope.

### Challenges And Root Causes

- The product smoke had to use a second documentation-view pass to prove sheet selector stability
  because the accepted default Cabinet view does not expose a sheet selector. That is a legitimate
  proof split, but it must remain documented so future reviewers do not think M31 changed the
  default view.
- Generated product/lib output and old examples create noisy scan results. Closeout scans must
  exclude build output and reference mirrors while still checking active source, docs, examples, and
  tests.
- Some compatibility paths are outside M31 acceptance but still real debt. Treating them as either
  "done" or "forgotten" would be inaccurate; they need deferred ownership.

### Key Lessons

1. Customer proof must be executable and structured. A screenshot without semantic proof is not
   enough for Athena.
2. Every retained compatibility path needs owner, reason, target, and verification before sprint
   close.
3. Final purge must check story evidence quality, not only code. A done story without AC evidence or
   cleanup notes is not done.
4. M31's product surface is Theia Graphical View; non-Theia mutation surfaces are legitimate future
   alignment work, not hidden M31 completion.
