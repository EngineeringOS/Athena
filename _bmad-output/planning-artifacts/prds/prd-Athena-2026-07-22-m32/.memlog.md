updated: 2026-07-22T15:39

- (decision) M32 selects Engineering Package And Representation Platform Foundation over Semantic Agent Runtime or symbol-count expansion because M31 exposed extension ecosystem and visual-resource boundaries as the next product gap.
- (decision) M32 separates Engineering Packages from Representation Packages; manifests and policy bridge them without leaking graphics into engineering semantics.
- (decision) M32 treats SVG, PNG, and future drawing resources as renderer resources behind Representation Descriptors, never as semantic authority or .athena syntax.
- (decision) Review accepted: rename M32 to Engineering Package Platform Foundation because representation is the first package family, not the whole platform.
- (decision) Review accepted: promote Presentation Profile and Binding Resolver as independent policy layer between Engineering Package and Representation Package.
